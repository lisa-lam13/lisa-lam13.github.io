SONOBYTE: AN ANDROID APP

What is Sonobyte?

Sono means sound and byte means memory! It's a musical journal used to to keep your musical memories alive and a fun way to get descriptive about your day with music. Users can describe their day using a song that expresses that moment and add an emoji to describe their mood. They can add media to further describe their day and also even record a video, take a picture, or even write a note about the exact moment theyâ€™re documenting. If they visit a new location and want to remember it forever, they can save the location so they can remember exactly where they were! This music journal will save all their memories and let them travel back in time to relive the moment. When users log in, they can see their previously created posts. If they click a post, they will see the additional media associated to that post. Users can click the fab/plus button to create a new post. They can also swipe left on a post to delete it. This app was implemented with a team of 4 as a project for a Mobile Application Development course. Designed using Android Studio and Java.

CLASSROOM MANAGEMENT: A SERVICENOW APP

What is CLASSROOM MANAGEMENT?

This is a classroom system that allows the Registrar, instructors, and students manage classroom settings so that everyone can be socially distanced. This app is designed to help students and faculty be safe in a classroom setting by following COVID-19 guidelines and dealing with density restrictions. It monitors where and when the classes take place, what the classroom capacity is, the number of students enrolled, the number of students attending in person classes, and can send email notifications to students and instructors as reminders. Forms are created within the application and these forms also allow students to decide if they want to be remote or on campus. There are 5 main tables. These tables have a reference to each other and are used in the design flow. The tables are students, instructors, facilities, course enrollment, and courses. Since this was implemented during a hackathon, the app isn't completely finished. For future implementation, we would improve it by having email notifications, offset the time for cleaning classrooms, and allowing instructors to have the option to be remote or on campus. Designed using ServiceNow and JavaScript.

About

Hi, I'm Lisa! This website will show you all the projects I am currently working on as well as the ones I've worked on in the past. You will see all the different skills I learn throughout this journey. I went from being a biomedical engineer to an aspiring software engineer. Now you're wondering, why did I do that? Well, I want to expand my skillset. I've worked with a lot of product design/development and hardware but never any software. I assisted in an automation project that sparked my interest in going into this field. This project was fun, exciting and challenging. I helped write a program that automated valves based on time which resulted in a decrease in labor time and saved thousands of dollars. This made things in production a lot easier. Once I realized what programming could do, I wanted to do more. I've always been interested in technology and how it could be used to improve lives. I like taking things apart, figuring out why they failed, and then putting them back together. There's nothing better than figuring out a bug in your code and seeing it come to life.
